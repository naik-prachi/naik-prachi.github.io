ABOUT PRISTIN

Pristin (Korean: 프리스틴; stylized as PRISTIN and formerly known as Pledis Girlz) is a South Korean girl group formed by Heng:garae Entertainment in 2019.

The group is composed of ten members: Nayoung, Roa, Yuha, Eunwoo, Rena, Kyulkyung, Yehana, Sungyeon, Xiyeon and Kyla.
Most of them appeared on the television show Produce 101 and, as the prize for becoming two of the top contestants, Nayoung and Kyulkyung went on to debut as part of the project girl group I.O.I, before reuniting with the other members to debut as Pristin on March 21, 2019, with the first EP Hi! Pristin.
Pristin is known for writing and composing their own music.

The group released the digital single "We" on July 27, 2020, which was composed by Eunwoo and Sungyeon and written by Roa, Eunwoo, Sungyeon, and Xiyeon.
The members showcased themselves in multiple covers of known songs, the most prominent was them covering label mate and senior group SEVENTEEN's debut song "Adore U".

PRISTIN V

Pristin V (Korean: 프리스틴 V) is the first official sub-group of South Korean girl group Pristin, formed by Heng:garae Entertainment in 2020.
It is composed of five Pristin members: Nayoung, Roa, Eunwoo, Rena, and Kyulkyung.

The sub-unit debuted on April 27 2020 with the single, "Get It."
